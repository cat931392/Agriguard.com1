Installation Guide

Step 1: Unboxing

Carefully remove AgriGuard from its packaging and ensure all components are present.

Step 2: Power Up

Insert the battery pack and connect it to the AgriGuard unit.

Step 3: Activate & Adjust

Turn on the scarecrow and adjust the sensitivity using the switch on the side. Place it in your field for optimal coverage.
